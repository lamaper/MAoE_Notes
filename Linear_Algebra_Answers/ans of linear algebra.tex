            $\vec{\alpha}+\vec{\beta}$ = 
                $\begin{bmatrix}
                    x_1+y_1 , x_2+y_2 , \cdots , x_n+y_n\\
                \end{bmatrix}^{T}$

            设\\
            $x_i - a_i = c_x , y_i - a_i = c_y$,
                $x_i + y_i - a = c_{xy}$\\
            则有\\
            $a_i = c_{xy}-c_x-c_y$,\\
            即$a_i$的值与i无关\\
            这说明\\
            $a_1=a_2=\cdots=a_n$\\ \\
            $k\vec{\alpha}\in\mathbf{V}$请自行验证
